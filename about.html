The Crispy King Story

Founded in 2020, Crispy King began with a simple mission: to serve the crispiest, most delicious fried chicken in East London.

Our Beginning

What started as a family recipe passed down through generations has evolved into East London's go-to spot for quality fried chicken. Our secret blend of spices and cooking techniques creates that perfect crunch in every bite.

Quality First

We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced from local suppliers. Our chicken is always halal-certified and prepared fresh daily. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Fresh Ingredients
